Overview

News and important dates

For the academic year 2024/2025, Communication Science students have the opportunity to study at various partner universities of the Chair of Communication Science in other European countries as part of the ERASMUS program. Please take note of the following important dates:

  • 05.11.2024 - Presentation as part of the International Day
  • 15.11.2024 - Activation of the online application
  • 05.12.2024 - Application deadline
